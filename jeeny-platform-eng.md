About the job

About Jeeny:

Jeeny is a mobile application that eases daily commuting and transportation. By connecting you with your preferred mode of transportation, we are fulfilling our aim of making mobility accessible, affordable, and flexible for all.

We are a joint venture between MEIG (Middle East Internet Group), Rocket Internet, and IMENA. Jeeny was established in 2014 as Easy Taxi. However, in 2016, it was revamped as Jeeny to cater to other services. Currently, we are operational in Saudi Arabia and Jordan.

We have offices in Riyadh, Jeddah, Madinah, Dammam, Khobar, Amman, Lahore, and Karachi.

Job Brief:

We are hiring a Platform Engineer to design and operate a scalable internal platform for our ride-hailing ecosystem. You will enable high-performance, secure, and reliable systems while empowering developers with self-service tools and streamlined workflows.

Responsibilities:

    Design, build, and maintain the Internal Developer Platform (IDP) to enable self-service infrastructure and standardized development workflows
    Develop and manage scalable, secure, and cost-efficient infrastructure on AWS, including Kubernetes (EKS) environments
    Implement and maintain Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform to ensure consistency and automation across all environments
    Build, optimize, and standardize CI/CD pipelines to support fast, reliable, and low-risk deployments
    Improve system reliability by implementing monitoring, logging, and alerting solutions (e.g., Prometheus, Grafana, ELK)
    Define and enforce best practices for scalability, performance, and high availability across services
    Collaborate with Security teams to integrate DevSecOps practices, including secrets management, IAM policies, and compliance requirements
    Enable development teams with self-service tools, templates, and documentation to reduce dependency on DevOps
    Drive cost optimization initiatives across cloud infrastructure and Kubernetes workloads
    Troubleshoot production issues, conduct root cause analysis, and improve system resilience
    Continuously enhance developer experience (DX) by simplifying workflows, reducing friction, and improving onboarding processes
    Stay up to date with emerging technologies and recommend improvements to platform architecture

Requirements

    Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience)
    5+ years of total experience, 1 year of experience in Platform Engineering/development, 4+ year DevOps, or SRE roles
    Proven experience working in cloud-native and microservices-based environments
    Strong hands-on experience with AWS (EKS, EC2, RDS, S3, IAM)
    Solid expertise in Kubernetes and container orchestration
    Experience with Infrastructure as Code tools such as Terraform
    Proficiency in CI/CD tools (GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, Jenkins)
    Strong understanding of Docker and containerization concepts
    Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack)
    Familiarity with databases such as MongoDB and MySQL
    Scripting/programming skills in Python, Bash, or Go
    Strong understanding of system design, scalability, and high availability
    Experience in automation and process optimization
    Knowledge of security best practices
